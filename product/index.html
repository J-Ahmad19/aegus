USBot — The Offline AI Powerhouse

USBot is the premier offline AI assistant designed for professionals who handle sensitive information. Unlike traditional cloud-based AI, USBot operates entirely on your local hardware.

It eliminates the risks of data breaches, third-party monitoring, and internet dependency — delivering a seamless, secure, and private environment for your most critical workflows.

Zero-Cloud Connectivity

All processing happens directly on your device. Your data never leaves your machine, ensuring total immunity from external leaks and unauthorized access.

Local Knowledge Retrieval

Upload your internal documents, legal briefs, or research papers. USBot uses advanced Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to provide answers strictly based on your data.

Professional-Grade Accuracy

Fine-tuned for technical, legal, and medical terminology, USBot delivers precise summaries, drafting assistance, and analysis you can trust.

Instant-On Accessibility

Because it operates offline, USBot remains available 24/7 — even in high-security environments or remote locations without internet access.
